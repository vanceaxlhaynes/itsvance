I spend most of my days with fleeting thoughts. Writing. Reading. And sitting with silence long enough to hear what it has to say. The morning walks keep me on a hill of quiet. I go weeks without noise. This is a small corner of the internet where words are left to breathe. Poetry, prose, and the occasional thought written in the hours between the noise. There's beauty in keeping things. And always faith in the quiet. Thank you for finding this place. - Vance
